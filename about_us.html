Spotify is a proprietary Swedish audio streaming and media services provider founded on 23 April 2006 by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon. It is one of the largest music streaming service providers, with over 422 million monthly active users, including 182 million paying subscribers, as of March 2022.
With Spotify, you can play and listen to millions of songs and podcasts for free! Listen to artists you love and enjoy music and playlists The basic, ad-supported version of Spotify is completely free. It offers unlimited access to the full catalog of music and podcasts but comes with a few drawbacks. The most intrusive are frequent ad breaks.
You can get a Spotify Premium account for free when you become a new subscriber. The trial lasts for 30 days, but it will automatically renew after a month and charge the card on file.
